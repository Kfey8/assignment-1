Negative Reinforcment Training

Kamryn Fey CS4843

Negative reinforcement is taking something away to encourage or reinforce a behavior. This type of reinforcement is used by any equestrian who rides on top of their horse. When turning the horse, you are pulling the horses head to the place you want to go. This takes away the ability for the horse to go another direction, and gets release when they are going the direction you asked to go.
